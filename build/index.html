Thomas A. Plein Foundation

The TAP FOUNDATION'S philosophy is rooted in the values, sentiments, and beliefs of the Foundation's founder Thomas A. Plein and supports the following:

  1. The efforts of the Catholic Church in the area of spiritual development, contemplative works, and the open-minded thought for change and growth in the Catholic Church.
  2. Support health care service organizations specifically to provide financial support for individuals needing health care but have demonstrated the financial need to secure such service.
  3. Support educational institutions specifically to provide financial support for individuals who have demonstrated financial need to attend said educational institution and who have demonstrated excellent academic achievement under distressed conditions.
  4. Support organizations not fitting criteria 1, 2 or 3, that are supported by a current board member and whereas the mission of the organization intersects with the specific purposes of the TAP FOUNDATION.
